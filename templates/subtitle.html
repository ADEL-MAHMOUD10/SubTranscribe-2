How to Use SRT and VTT Files

On PC

To use SRT or VTT files on your PC:

  1. Download the SRT or VTT file from the website.
  2. Open your media player (like VLC or Windows Media Player).
  3. Load your video file.
  4. Go to the subtitle settings and choose "Add Subtitle File".
  5. Select the downloaded subtitle file.

Enjoy your video with subtitles!

On Mobile

To use SRT or VTT files on your mobile device:

  1. Download the SRT or VTT file from the website.
  2. Open your video player app (like MX Player or VLC for Mobile).
  3. Load your video file within the app.
  4. Tap on the subtitle icon or settings.
  5. Select "Load Subtitle" and choose the downloaded file.

Now you can watch your video with subtitles on the go!
